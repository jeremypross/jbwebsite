About The Prints

We present these images in two types of prints:

Black & White Silver Gelatin Prints

All black & white images are available from the Galleries listed below in silver gelatin prints three sizes, 11x14 inches, 16x20 inches and 20x24 inches. These are exhibition quality, archivally processed, selenium-toned silver gelatin fiber-based photographs. The production of each and every print is personally supervised and approved by the photographer before being captioned and signed in pencil on the reverse; buyers may also request a signature below the image on the front surface.

  • 11 x 14" Prints: Unnumbered Open Edition
  • 16 x 20" Prints: Numbered, Limited Edition of 75 Prints
  • 20 x 24" Prints: Numbered, Limited Edition of 50 Prints

Black & White and Color Digital Prints:

All color images and a select number of black & white images are available as large, high quality, archival digital prints only from the Morrison Hotel Gallery and the San Francisco Art Exchange (see below).

All black & white digital prints offered for sale are printed on a Canon iPF8300 archival inkjet printer using Canon Lucia EX pigment inks, on either Premium BC Vibrance Baryta or Hahnemuhle Photo Rag Baryta paper. According to according to Wilhelm Research of Grinnell, Iowa, the expert on the subject, these prints will resist noticeable fading for over 250 years when framed and under glass. All color digital prints offered for sale are estimated to resist fading for at least 100 years.

Both black & white and color digital prints are produced in the following signed editions:

Color Digital Prints:
  • 20 x 30" Prints: Numbered, Limited Edition of 50 Prints
Black & White Digital Prints
  • 30 x 40" Prints: Numbered, Limited Edition of 25 Prints
